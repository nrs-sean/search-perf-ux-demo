UX takeaway

For server-backed typeahead, 150–250 ms is usually a good starting point. Past ~300 ms of debounce, users begin to feel the delay; with high server latency, even a “good” debounce may feel slow.

What this demonstrates

Debounce delay and server latency compound. Debounce is the intentional wait before posting; server latency is the unavoidable wait after posting. The user feels the sum of both.
